**Water Purification**
Boil for at least 1 minute or use purification tablets.

**Food Storage**
Store dry goods in sealed containers. Keep canned food in a cool, dry place.

**First Aid**
Always have a stocked first aid kit with antiseptic, bandages, and pain relief.

**Shelter**
Use tarps, mylar blankets, or natural resources to create temporary shelters.

**Fire Making**
Use a firestarter, waterproof matches, or create friction-based fires.

**Navigation**
Use a compass and maps. Learn to navigate using the sun and stars.

**Barter and Trade**
Identify barter-friendly communities. Offer useful skills or tradeable items.

**Emergency Communication**
Use HAM radios, CB radios, or encrypted messaging tools like Briar.
